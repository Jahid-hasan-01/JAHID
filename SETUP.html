üéØ Getting Started (3 Steps)

Step 1: Open Your Website

Simply open index.html in your web browser. That's it! The site is fully functional right out of the box.

No server needed to see it work!

Step 2: Customize Your Content

Edit index.html and replace:

  • Your name and professional title
  • About section text
  • Work experience (companies, dates, descriptions)
  • Services you offer
  • Your portfolio projects
  • Contact information

Step 3: Personalize the Design

Edit css/custom.css to change:

  • Colors: Look for --primary-color: #22c55e and change to your brand color
  • Fonts: Modify Google Fonts import for different typography
  • Spacing: Adjust padding and margins to your preference

üì∏ Adding Your Images

To replace placeholder images:

  1. Place your images in the assets/images/ folder
  2. Find the image placeholders in index.html
  3. Replace placeholders with: <img src="assets/images/your-image.jpg" alt="Description">

‚úâÔ∏è Contact Form Setup

The contact form currently shows success messages but doesn't send emails.

Option A: Use Formspree (Easiest)

  1. Go to formspree.io
  2. Create a free account and add your form
  3. Copy your form ID
  4. In index.html, find the contact form and change:
    <form class="contact-form" id="contactForm">
    to:
    <form action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">

Option B: Use EmailJS (No Backend)

  1. Visit emailjs.com
  2. Sign up free and get your Service ID
  3. Add the EmailJS library to index.html:
    <script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@emailjs/browser@3/dist/index.min.js"></script>
